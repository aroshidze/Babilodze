How to Play

1. You are Coach Babilodze. Your team is in RED.

2. One of your players starts with the ball (indicated by a white glow).

3. Click and drag from the player with the ball to a teammate to draw a pass.

4. Continue drawing lines from teammate to teammate to create a passing sequence.

5. To shoot, draw the final line from a player into the opponent's goal.

6. Release the mouse button to execute the play.

7. Avoid the opponent defenders in BLUE. If they intercept a pass or block a shot, you lose.

8. Score a goal to complete the level and unlock the next tactical problem!
